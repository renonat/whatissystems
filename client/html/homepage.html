Welcome to

Systems Design Engineering

at the University of Waterloo

Systems Design Engineering is an interdisciplinary program, with a focus on solving big problems. Students tackle complex physical systems, with a focus on the human factors involved.

Students, alumni, and their employers love Systems Design Engineering. But to an outsider, the program is misunderstood and poorly explained. Even our own Dean of Engineering was reportedly quoted saying, "I don't know what systems design engineering is". This website exists to mend these issues.
