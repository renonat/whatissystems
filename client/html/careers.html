Career Possibilities

Systems Design Engineering imparts a highly valued skill-set that can be applied in nearly any field. This allows for large flexibility in choosing a career path.

Types of Jobs

Depending on your interests and skills, you can work in almost any position and industry as a Systems Design Engineer. These are the most common co-op positions:

  • Software Developer: Designing the architecture for, and programming, the next generation of computer, mobile, and decentralized applications.
  • UI/UX & Designer: Designing and optimizing products for human use, while considering their visual, tactile, and functional appeal.
  • Data Scientist: Extracting knowledge and insights from the ever growing amount of data in the world.
  • Project Manager: Be responsible for the planning, procurement, and execution of a project, from start to finish.
  • Product Manager: Combine technical, design, data, and business expertise to define the features of a product and oversee production.
