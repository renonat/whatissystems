Shadow Days

Shadow Days are day-in-the-life tour with a current Waterloo Engineering student. You can request an ambassador in SYDE and spend a day with us on campus. Attending classes with real students is a fantastic way to see if the program will be a good fit for you.

Waterloo Engineering Shadow Program

Open Houses

Open Houses are another great way to meet current students and tour campus. Not only will you find a booth featuring SYDE students and professors, but can interact with student design teams and societies.
